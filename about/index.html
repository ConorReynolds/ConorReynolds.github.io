I am a PhD student at Maynooth University. My research interests include the theory of institutions, the Coq proof assistant, Event-B, and formal software design in general.

My PhD research1 involves formalising the theory of institutions in Coq, with the institution for Event-B2 as a primary use-case, supervised by Dr Rosemary Monahan. The formalisation is available on my GitHub.
