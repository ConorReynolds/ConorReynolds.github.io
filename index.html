I am a PhD student at Maynooth University. My research interests include the theory of institutions, the Coq proof assistant, the Event-B formal modelling tool, and formal software design in general.

I’m working on formalising the theory of institutions in Coq1, with the institution for Event-B2 as a primary use-case—supervised by Dr Rosemary Monahan. The formalisation is open source and available on my GitHub.
